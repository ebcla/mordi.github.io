What’s happening

We’re starting at Mordialloc Pier, swimming along the water for roughly 1.2km, then heading to The Bridge Hotel for a pub meal.

  • Friendly pace — no stress
  • We’ll regroup at key points
  • Meal after for anyone who wants to join (swimmers + non-swimmers welcome)
